Wurm Vein Constraint Solver

Paste your shard/Analyse output. Tell the tool how far you stepped (E/W and N/S) from the original paste. After each add, the map regenerates. If a vein resolves to exactly one tile, it will lock and never move.

1) Add step + pasted log

E/W (x)
N/S (y)
Convention: East = +x, West = −x, North = +y, South = −y.
Tip: paste only the relevant lines; the parser ignores unrelated text.

2) View

“ALL” shows combined; select a vein key to inspect its feasible tiles.
Expected text formats
  • You find faint traces of iron (north of east).
  • You find a trace of silver (west).
  • You would mine iron here. (treated as “exact tile”)
  • Direction supports: N, S, E, W, NE, NW, SE, SW, and “north of east”, “east of north”, etc.
